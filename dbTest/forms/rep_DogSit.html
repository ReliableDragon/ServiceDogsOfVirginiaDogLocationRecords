perfection. However, we want to know what they are doing in a novel home and this
information can tell us what we need to work on so your feedback is important. Don't be shy
about telling us what the dog did with you. Remember to have reasonable expectations
based on the dog's age and what you were told in the survey you got when you agreed to
watch this trainee. THANK YOU!!
